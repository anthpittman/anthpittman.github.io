About Mr. Pittman

My name is Mr. Anthony Pittman. I am the Lead for Ayala's Academy of Computer and Engineering Sciences (ACES) and co-Chair of the Linked Learning Academies program. The ACES academy focuses on programming, robotics, and automation. I currently teach AP CSPand Principles of Engineering at Ruben S Ayala High School. Some facts about Mr. Pittman:

  • Graduated with a Bachelor's from UC San Diego
  • Obtained a Master's in Technology Teaching from NU
  • Worked in the Information Technology industry for 8 years
  • Taught Advanced Placement courses for 9 years
